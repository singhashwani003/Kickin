KICKIN: Sustainable Footwear for a Healthier Planet

KICKIN, a sustainable footwear brand, prioritizes eco-friendly materials and ethical production, minimizing their carbon footprint. It reinforces their commitment, emphasizing the important of environmentally-conscious products in meeting consumer demands and promoting a healthier planet.

Kickin was founded in 2023 by a group of passionate shoe enthusiasts who recognized a gap in the market for stylish, high-quality footwear that was both affordable and sustainable. The founders came from diverse backgrounds in fashion, design, and environmental science, and they shared a common goal: to create a shoe brand that not only looked good but also felt good to wear and was kind to the planet.
